Biography

I am a Masters student in the Electrical Engineering Department at School of Electrical Engineering and Computer Science (SEECS), NUST.

My broad fields of interest are Machine Learning, Deep Learning and Autonomous Systems. Recently, I have also developed a new found interest towards Reinforcement Learning, and I am currently exploring this area.

I was a Research Intern at the TUKL Deep Learning Lab, NCAI, Islamabad, in the summer of 2021, where I worked in the area of Natural Language Processing and Automatic Summarization. Apart from this, I have done numerous projects on areas like Home Energy Management using Deep RL, Semi-Autonomous Weed Sprayer Robot, Abstractive and Extractive Legal Document Summarisation and others. An exhaustive detailed list of my projects can be found in the Projects Section.

In my free time, I love acquire advanced techs skills from Online platforms, and watching movies. I am also a gaming enthusiast, playing numerous open world games from time to time.
